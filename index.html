Thank you for staying. Thank you for choosing us, even on the ordinary days. Thank you for standing beside me through moments that were easy, and even more through the ones that werent.

Thank you for always believing that we can become something better together. One of the things i admire most about you is that youre always trying to grow.. not because i asked you to, and not because you think youre not enough, but because you genuinely want the best for us. That means more to me than i could ever put into these words.

Youve taught me that love isnt just found in grand gestures. Sometimes its hidden in patience, in understanding, in checking on each other after a long day, in listening, in forgiving, and in choosing each other again and again.

I’m grateful for every version of you sayang. The happy one. The tired one. The overthinking one. The clingy one. or even the quiet one that doesnt always know what to say. Every version of you deserves to be loved, and i hope you never doubt that.

I dont know what tomorrow looks like. i dont know how many pages our story still has left. But i do know one thing.

If i get to keep writing those pages with you, i will always consider myself the luckiest man alive.
